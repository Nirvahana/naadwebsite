Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 4, 2026

Naad is designed as a calm, personal spiritual practice space.

We value simplicity and privacy.

This policy explains what information is collected and how it is used.

Information We Collect

Practice Data

Naad stores your mantra practice information, including:

  • • Mantra selections
  • • Japa counts (number of repetitions)
  • • Sankalp participation
  • • Session history

This data is used only to provide app functionality.

Custom Mantras

If you choose to create a custom mantra, it is stored locally on your device.

Naad does not analyze, interpret, or share the content of custom mantras.

Custom mantra data is used only to support your personal practice.

Device Information

Basic technical information may be collected automatically, such as:

  • • Device type
  • • Operating system version
  • • App version
  • • Crash logs (if enabled)

This helps improve stability and performance.

What We Do NOT Collect

Naad does not collect:

  • • Handwriting data or stroke patterns
  • • Personal identity information
  • • Location data
  • • Contact lists or photos

We do not:

  • • Sell personal data
  • • Share spiritual practice data with advertisers
  • • Track your behavior across other apps
  • • Require account creation

Analytics

If analytics are used, they are:

  • • Aggregate and anonymous
  • • Used only to improve stability and user experience
  • • Never used for advertising or cross-app tracking

Sankalp Participation

Community Sankalp participation is:

  • • Optional
  • • Reversible
  • • Anonymous

If collective counts are displayed, they are aggregated totals and do not reveal personal identity.

Data Storage

Currently:

  • • Most data is stored locally on your device
  • • Your practice data remains private and offline-first
  • • We do not use invasive tracking tools

Future updates may introduce optional cloud sync. If that occurs, this policy will be updated and you will be notified.

Children's Privacy

Naad does not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

If you believe a child has submitted personal data, please contact us at support@naad108.com.

Changes to This Policy

As Naad evolves, this policy may be updated.

Significant changes will be clearly communicated through the app.

Contact

For privacy-related questions:

Email: support@naad108.com

Website: naad108.com
